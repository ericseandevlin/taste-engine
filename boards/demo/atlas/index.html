Swiss Index 6

accent Minimal — hairline rules, small numerals, metadata labels; color used sparingly if at all
photo Documentary or object photography cropped flush to grid; no bleed chaos
space Structured negative space driven by column math; breathing room is deliberate not accidental

forbidden: Decorative or display typefaces · Asymmetric blob layouts · High-saturation color fields · Overlapping or collaged elements · Gradient backgrounds · Centered hero compositions
